Other Ways to Support the PTO

There are many ways to support the PTO. We appreciate any support you can give, whether it is monetary or by volunteering for some of our activities. The PTO depends on you.

Register Your Shopping Cards

Busch's: Instructions can be found at http://www.buschs.com/community.

Kroger:Link your Kroger Rewards card at Kroger's. You need to re-enroll your card every Spring! Check your receipts to check your registration.

Arbor Farms: Shop at Arbor Farms and, when you are checking out, just tell the cashier that you want to support Wines Elementary. Arbor Farms will donate 3% of your purchase total to support our school. Your grocery receipt will show "Wines Elementary School" on the top.

Amazon Smile

Support Wines by using AmazonSmile when making purchases from Amazon. Using AmazonSmile, gives you the same prices and selection as the regular site; with the added bonus that Amazon will donate 0.5% to the PTO. For your purchases to support our PTO, you MUST start your order at the smile.amazon.com and select Wines PTO as the charity of choice. AmazonSmile

 

Save Box Tops for Education

You can also save your Boxtops for Education labels and bring them to school. It helps us out if you can trim the edges!

Shop the PTO Thrift Shop

Four times a year, Wines participates in Show Your Support fundraisers. If you shop at the PTO Thrift Shop during SYS weeks and spend at least $5, you'll earn a token that you can put in the Wines jar in the lobby. The more tokens we earn, the larger the proportion of the $1,000 prize we earn.

 

Use our Square Marketplace

Visit our Square Marketplace to make a direct donation to the PTO.
